Co-curricular Records and Certificate Registry

Welcome to the Co-curricular Records and Certificate Registry DApp. This decentralized app runs on the Ropsten TestNet blockchain network and holds records of co-curricular activities and registry of certificates as Solidity smart contract.
  • The registry keeps records and hashes of certificates along with their publish date.
  • The certificates are stored on IPFS.
  • Contract owner can submit new records and certificates to be stored on the blockchain.
  • Users can view the records and certificates.
